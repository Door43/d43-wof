====== purple ======

===== Facts: =====

A highly valuable color of the Middle East.

  * Among the Hebrews purple clothing was considered a sign of wealth, distinction and royalty.
  * Purple robes were worn by the Kings of Israel's surrounding pagan nations.
  * Jesus was clad in a purple robe at His crucifixtion as a mockery to his claim of being King of the Jews. Mark 15:17
