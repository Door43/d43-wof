====== thief ======

===== Facts: =====

A thief is a person who steals another person's money, goods or property without their consent.

  * Instead of engaging in gainful employment to get money or goods a thief takes what rightfully belongs to another person.
  * In the New Testament Satan is described as a thief who comes to steal, kill and destroy. This illustration shows Satan's destructive agenda in people's lives.
  * Thieves often use an element of surprise, lurking and watching for opporune moments to take things. Often they use the cover of darkness to help them with stealth.
