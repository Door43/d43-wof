====== locust ======

===== Facts: =====

Locust are large straight-winged insects that live in trees mainly in more tropical places that make chirping sounds with its back legs. 
 
  * Locusts were one of the ten plagues sent by God to free the Israelites from captivity in Egypt. 
  * All through the Old Testament locusts are seen as a plague and infestation that destroy crops and animals. 
  * The New Testament says that locusts were a main food source for John the Baptist.
